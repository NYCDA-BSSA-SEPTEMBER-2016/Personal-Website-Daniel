About Me

Bio

As so many little kids I was fascinated by flying and back in those days I didn't want to become anything more then a pilot. I was imagining myself soring through the skies, but at one point it suddenly hit me...

What if I could invent these amazing things that enabled us how to fly. This thought turned my world upside down, my new heroes suddenly became inventors and scientists in the likes of Leonardo da Vinci, Einstein and Edison. Just like them I wanted to change the world and invent things that could better the lives of everyone on this planet.

Now many years later I still have this passion for technology, there is no moment that passes when I am not fascinated by the speed that everything around me is changing. I am still the little kid, although a little taller by now...
